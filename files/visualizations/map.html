Record density map

Type: Map, equirectangular projection

Applies to: Publisher and collection

Interactive: No

Description:
Record density map over an equirectangular projection. This projection makes it easy to plot coordinates stored in decimal degree system. Each dot is a unique combination of latitude and longitude values. Color represents number of records falling on that point over a cold-hot binary scale, being dark blue equal to 1 or 2 records and brown representing more than 2048 records.
