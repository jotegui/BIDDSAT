Record density for each day of year

Type: Hebdoplot

Applies to: Publisher and collection

Interactive: Yes

Description:
Record density table, where each cell is a unique day of the year, and cell value is the total amount of records falling in that day. The greener a cell is, the more records that fall in that day. This plot is useful for detecting seasonal patterns, specific day-range campaigns or other date biases. By selecting the "content" of the cells, actual values are shown.
